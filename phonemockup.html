How to Create Your Mockup

1. Choose Model

Select a phone style or layout from the dropdown menu above. (Note: These are not real phone names, just for identification.)

2. Set Background

Pick a solid color, a gradient, or upload your own image for the background.

3. Upload Media

Click on the phone's screen to upload your image or video file.

4. Download

Click the "Download" button and choose your desired format and quality.
